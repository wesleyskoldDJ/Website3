About

WesKey is a record producer based in Greenville, South Carolina. He specializes in isolating a song or artist’s core vibe, then building a track with elements that complement and highlight that vibe. Instead of applying a stock sound to every track, he takes the time to unlock what the song is doing so that every piece of the production carries forth the song’s core. That’s the WesKey.

WesKey attended the University of Pennsylvania, studying music and becoming fluent in Spanish and Portuguese. After college he remained in Philadelphia, where the rich bedrock of Neo Soul, Gospel, and R&B gave life to his music career. While in Philly, he worked with such artists as Jahmena, Peri Jon and David P. Stevens. His 2017 collaboration with David P. Stevens, “For The Win” earned the artist his first top 10 and a sixteen-week stay on the Billboard Smooth Jazz Top 30 chart.

His DAW of choice is Logic for its ability to provide both a fluid space for track-building as well as a professional-grade environment for recording and mixing.

As a listener, WesKey enjoys all sorts of genres including Neo-Soul, mid-late 90’s/early 2000’s Hip Hop, R&B, alt-pop, Pop, Bebop, Latin Jazz, Latin Pop, Afro Pop, House, and more.
